At Takshashila Homes, we offer a comprehensive range of design services across architecture, interiors, furniture, lighting, product design, and landscaping. Guided by the impeccable style and vision of Takshashila Home herself, our dedicated team creates forward-thinking and visually striking projects. We conceive and execute harmonious designs that seamlessly blend functionality with aesthetics, ensuring each project is uniquely tailored to our clients' lifestyles.

Interiors

Our Takshashila services are tailored to transform your spaces into reflections of your personality and lifestyle. We start with an in-depth consultation to understand your preferences, needs, and aspirations.
Our services include space planning, colour schemes, material selection, and the integration of custom furniture and decor. We pay close attention to every detail, ensuring that each room is both beautiful and functional, creating a cohesive and inviting environment throughout your home.

Lighting

Lighting is a critical component of any design, affecting both the functionality and ambiance of a space. Our lighting design services include the careful selection and placement of fixtures to create the perfect atmosphere for each room.
We consider factors such as natural light, room usage, and aesthetic preferences to develop a lighting plan that enhances your interiors, highlighting architectural features and setting the desired mood.

04  

Product Design

Our product design team creates unique, innovative pieces that add a distinctive touch to your home. From custom fixtures and hardware to decorative accessories and one-of-a-kind art pieces, our products blend form and function seamlessly.
We work closely with skilled artisans and craftsmen to ensure that each item meets our high standards of quality and design, providing you with exclusive elements that elevate your space.

05 

Landscaping

The exterior of your home is the first impression it makes, and our landscaping services ensure it’s a stunning one. We design outdoor spaces that are both beautiful and functional, creating gardens, patios, and living areas that extend the comfort and style of your interiors.
Our landscaping plans consider the local climate, topography, and your personal preferences to create sustainable, low-maintenance environments that you can enjoy year-round.

Decor

At Takshashila Homes, our décor services are designed to add the perfect finishing touches to your interiors, reflecting your unique style and personality. We offer custom décor solutions, including bespoke artwork, unique accessories, and custom-designed soft furnishings, all tailored to enhance your space.
Our styling and staging services ensure a polished, cohesive look in every room, with expertly arranged furniture and décor that create beautiful and functional spaces.
