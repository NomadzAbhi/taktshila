About Us

One of the great beauties of architecture is that each time, it is like life starting all over again.

Nishma Sharma is a visionary interior designer with an illustrious career spanning 19 years. Nishma has established herself as a beacon of excellence in the field of Takshashila. Her portfolio is a testament to her versatility, encompassing a wide array of projects in hospitality, commercial, and residential sectors.

Nishma’s vision is to transform spaces into reflections of human aspirations, creating environments that resonate with elegance, functionality, and sustainability. She believes that every space should tell a story, evoke emotions, and enhance the quality of life for those who inhabit it.

Takshashila Home’s mission is to deliver unparalleled design solutions that blend aesthetics with purpose. She is committed to pushing the boundaries of creativity while ensuring that her designs are practical, sustainable, and tailored to the unique needs of each client. Her firm is dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of excellence and innovation in every project.

From hospitality projects that exude opulence and comfort, to innovative commercial spaces that foster productivity and creativity, to bespoke residential interiors that offer sanctuary and style, Nishma’s work is characterised by meticulous attention to detail and a relentless pursuit of perfection. Her ability to blend form and function, traditional and contemporary elements, and global influences with local sensibilities sets her apart in the industry.

Takshashila Home stands for more than just design; it stands for a commitment to excellence, a passion for innovation, and a dedication to enhancing the human experience through thoughtfully crafted spaces. With nearly two decades of experience, Nishma continues to inspire and redefine the boundaries of Takshashila, one project at a time.
